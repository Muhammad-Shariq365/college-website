Our Courses

Our College offers a diverse range of courses, from traditional academic subjects to specialized programs that cater to students' unique interests and career aspirations.Our experienced faculty members use a variety of teaching methods to deliver engaging and interactive lessons that challenge and inspire students to reach their full potential.

Information Technology

Information technology (IT) refers to the use and management of computers, software, networks, and electronic systems to store, process, transmit, and retrieve data, enabling efficient communication and automation of tasks in various fields.

Electronic Engineering

Electrical engineering focuses on designing, developing, and maintaining electrical systems, devices, and power networks. It involves studying circuits, electromagnetism, digital systems, and electronics for various applications use in daily life.

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering involves designing, constructing, and maintaining infrastructure like buildings, bridges, roads, and water systems. It encompasses areas such as structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering.

Manegerial Accounting

Managerial accounting focuses on providing financial information and analysis to support internal decision-making within organizations. It involves budgeting, cost analysis, performance evaluation, and strategic planning to help managers make informed business decisions.

Envormental Sciences

Environmental sciences encompass the study of interactions between the natural world and human activities, focusing on environmental issues, conservation, pollution, climate change, ecosystems, biodiversity, and sustainable practices to protect the Earth's resources for future generations..

Petroleum Engineering

Petroleum engineering involves the exploration, extraction, and production of oil and gas resources. It combines knowledge of geology, reservoir engineering, drilling, and production techniques to optimize extraction and maximize resource recovery in effective manner.
